**Author:** Joseph Albahari & Eric Johannsen

## üí° Reading Tips

1. **Don't read linearly**: Jump to chapters relevant to current work
2. **Take notes**: Summarize key points in your own words
3. **Apply immediately**: Try concepts in real projects
4. **Re-read**: Books reveal more on second reading
5. **Discuss**: Join book clubs or online discussions
6. **Mix old and new**: Classics provide foundations, new books cover modern practices
